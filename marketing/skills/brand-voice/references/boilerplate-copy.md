## Product descriptions

### Abeam Pilot (Free Tier)

**Primary message:** Abeam is the app that actually gets what you need as a pilot — your logbook, your currency, your weather, all in one place, on every device.

**Supporting messages:**

**Your logbook, everywhere you need it.** Abeam works on your iPhone, your iPad, and the web — so your data is always with you whether you're at the airport, at home, or on the go.

**Free to fly with, and we mean it.** No arbitrary hour caps. No bait-and-switch. Log flights for free and upgrade when the features make sense for you.

**Your currency, handled.** Upload your medical and certificates once. Abeam tracks your currency automatically and reminds you before anything expires.

**Live weather on your dashboard.** Pick your home airport, and see current METAR conditions every time you open the app. One fewer app to check.

**Your digital hangar.** Add and manage your aircraft — whether you rent, own, or share. Every tail number, every detail, integrated directly with your logbook.

**Built-in references.** FARs, AIM, and essential regulatory docs searchable and always in your pocket. No more Googling the regs on the ramp.

**Built by a pilot, not a committee.** Abeam was designed by someone who actually sits in the left seat.

### Abeam Instruct (Paid — Instructor Tools)

**Primary message:** Everything in Abeam Pilot, plus the tools to run your instruction the way it should be run.

**Supporting messages:**

**Student tracking and management.** See all your students, their progress, their recent flights, and what's next — in one dashboard.

**Your instructor resource portal.** Share documents, study materials, and tools directly with your students through Abeam. No more texting file attachments.

**Priced for a CFI budget.** You're building hours, not an empire. Abeam Instruct costs less than a single ground lesson.
