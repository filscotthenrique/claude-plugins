## Campaign / feature-specific lines

### Cross-platform

**Note:** Abeam is currently iOS + Web only. No Android app exists yet. Avoid any tagline that mentions Android or implies universal device support. Adjust these lines accordingly.

1. ~~"iPhone. Android. Web. Your logbook lives everywhere you do."~~ → Use: "iPhone. iPad. Web. Your logbook lives everywhere you do."
2. "Switch devices, not logbooks." (still works for iOS + Web)
3. ~~"Finally, a pilot app that doesn't care what phone you carry."~~ → Do not use (implies Android)
4. "Your data follows you. Not the other way around." (still works)
5. ~~"No ecosystem lock-in. Just your flights, everywhere."~~ → Do not use (overpromises on platform coverage)
