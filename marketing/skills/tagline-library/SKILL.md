## Taglines to avoid

Never use these patterns in Abeam copy:

- Anything with "OS," "operating system," or "ecosystem"
- "All-in-one" — overused SaaS cliche
- "Next-gen" or "reimagined" — buzzwords pilots distrust
- "For the modern pilot" — implies older pilots aren't welcome
- "Disrupting aviation" — no one in GA wants to hear this
- "The Uber of..." or "The [consumer brand] of flying" — cringe in aviation culture
- "Fly smarter" as a standalone — too vague without context
- "Revolutionize your flying" — oversells and undersells simultaneously

## How to use these taglines

- **Social posts**: Pick a tagline as the hook or closing line, then build the caption around it.
- **App Store**: The subtitle is the most important line — pick from the subtitle options above.
- **Ads**: Use campaign lines as headlines. Keep the brand tagline as the anchor.
- **Email subject lines**: Adapt campaign lines — they're already punchy and short.
- **Landing pages**: Use the brand tagline as the hero, supporting taglines for section headers.

Don't use taglines verbatim if they sound forced in context. Adapt the energy and angle, not the exact words.
